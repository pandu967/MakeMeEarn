About

This is about MakeMeEarn. This platform allows users to get financial freedom and can change their lives in the way they want to. The app can be used in a number of ways. It can be used by different organisations or household industries who need workers. It can be also used by women who are either skilled or unskilled but willing to work with interest.

Objective

There are lot of women who are facing economic challanges.Even though they are interested and capable to work due to lack of opportunities and support they lag behind.Through this web app we provide opportunity to women who are willing to work and who are in need of work.The main objective of our webapp is to provide economic freedom to women by giving them opportunity to stand on their own earnings.
